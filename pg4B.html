Dark-Mode

In a corner of a big busy city, there was a building that stood near a forest. There were many haunted stories regarding the building that after midnight, strange noises would be heard from it. The locals also claimed that after midnight people entering the building were seen but never to be found later. Anyone who entered the building never made it out. There were rumours that in the building lived a tall dark man who was said to have magical powers that normal people could not perform.
